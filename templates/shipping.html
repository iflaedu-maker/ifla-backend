Shipping & Delivery Policy

Most of our courses are delivered digitally. For any physical materials (if applicable), the following timelines apply within India:

  • Processing Time: 1–2 business days
  • Delivery Time (Metro): 2–5 business days
  • Delivery Time (Non-Metro): 3–7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5–10 business days

Tracking details will be shared via email where applicable.

For digital products, access is granted instantly upon successful payment.
